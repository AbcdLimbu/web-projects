Classic Maggie Noodles

Quick easy, and delicious - ready in 5 minutes!

Prep time: 2 minutes

Cook time: 5 minutes

Servings: 1 Person

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Maggie tastemaker (masala packet)
    • Contains: salt, spices, dehydrated veggies
  • Optional add-ons
    • 1 egg
    • Chopped Onions
    • Green Chilies
    • Butter (1 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add maggie noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Add a taste maker and stir well
  4. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Optional: Crack an egg in and stir quickly for egg Maggie.
  6. Remove from heat. Let it sit for 30 seconds.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Pro tips

  • Less water = thicker, dried Maggi
  • More water = soupy Maggi - great in winters!
  • Add butter at the end for extra richness
  • Never overcook - 2 minutes max!
